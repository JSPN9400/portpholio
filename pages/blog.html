Philosophy

"Data is not numbers. Data is human behaviour, recorded."

Every dataset is a record of decisions, habits, and choices made by real people. When we clean data, we're not just fixing errors — we're restoring the accuracy of a human record.

Business Intelligence

"The best KPI is the one that makes the right person uncomfortable at the right time."

A KPI that confirms what everyone already knows is just expensive decoration. The value of a metric is measured by how often it triggers a conversation that wouldn't have happened otherwise.

2 min read · Note→
Automation

"Automation isn't laziness. It's redirecting human attention from repetition to insight."

Every hour a analyst spends manually copying data between spreadsheets is an hour not spent asking "why did revenue drop in this region?" Automation is not about doing less — it's about doing better.
