## ⚠️ Mistakes to Avoid

- ❌ Don't use the same keywords everywhere (vary naturally)
- ❌ Don't buy backlinks (use white-hat methods only)
- ❌ Don't keyword stuff (focus on readability)
- ❌ Don't neglect mobile optimization
- ❌ Don't forget to monitor Search Console
- ❌ Don't update just once (consistency matters)
