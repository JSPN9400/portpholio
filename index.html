Professional Background

I am a Data Analyst with expertise in financial data operations, high-volume transaction processing, and KPI reporting. My focus is on extracting measurable business value through structured data analysis.

I work with SQL and Python to process and analyze large transactional datasets, identifying patterns that inform business strategy. I design Power BI dashboards that translate raw data into clear, decision-supporting metrics.

My approach emphasizes structured problem-solving: define the business question, execute precise queries, validate results, and communicate findings clearly. I prioritize accuracy and replicability in every analysis.

I have built reporting systems that track financial performance, revenue metrics, and operational KPIs. My experience spans data modeling, ETL processes, and developing frameworks that enable consistent insight generation.
